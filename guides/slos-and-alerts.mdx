# SLOs & alerts

Use the RED metrics (Rate, Errors, Duration) that avuru obs derives
automatically to define **service-level objectives** and alert on burn rate.

- Define an SLO per critical endpoint (e.g. 99.9% < 300 ms).
- Alert on **error-budget burn rate**, not raw thresholds.
