The **Services** screen is live: every service seen in the selected window —
auto-discovered zero-code on Kubernetes or sending OTLP directly — with its
rate, error percentage and latency percentiles in one sortable table.

{/* truncate */}

- **RED per service.** Request rate, error % (badge when non-zero) and
  p50/p95/p99, computed from the same span data the trace explorer uses.
- **Drill-down.** Click a row to open that service's traces, already filtered;
  or jump to the topology view of the same services on the service map.
- **Consistent filters.** The global time range and the auxiliary-traffic
  toggle work here like everywhere else, and the state lives in the URL — a
  filtered view is shareable.
