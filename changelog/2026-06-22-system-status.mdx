A new **System Status** tab (under Settings) gives operators an at-a-glance view
of the backend's health.

{/* truncate */}

- **Component health** — hub, ClickHouse and ingestion, with last-data freshness.
- **Storage per signal** — rows, on-disk size, compression ratio, data-since and
  retention (days) for traces and logs.
- **Disk capacity** — ClickHouse disk usage with a free/total bar.

Served by `/api/v1/system/status`, so any client can surface the same numbers.
